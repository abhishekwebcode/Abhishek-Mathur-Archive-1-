    Privacy Statement

    Updated July 24th, 2018
    Stealth Sonics (“Stealth Sonics”, “we”, “us” or the “Company”) recognizes the importance of privacy of visitors and users of www.stealthsonics.com (our “Website”). The following Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) is provided to inform you of Stealth Sonics’s policies and practices regarding the collection, retention, use, and disclosure of information we receive about you from the use of our Website.

    Information We Collect from You
    In general, you may visit the Stealth Sonics Website or contact Stealth Sonics by phone without providing your personal information.
    When you inquire as a prospective customer by email, you will be asked to provide your personal information such as your name, email address and phone number. When you register on our Website, or place an order through our Website, we collect your name, mailing address, phone number, email address, contact preferences and credit card information. We collect this information in order to process your order and to further contact you with respect to your order.
    We may also collect information about gift recipients such as the name, email address and mailing address to fulfill the gift purchase.
    Like many websites, we use “cookies” to recognize you and your device and gather information about visits to our Website to enhance your experience.

    How We Protect Your Personal Information
    We implement a variety of technical and organizational security measures to maintain the security of your personal data. Your personal data is contained behind secured networks and is accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems and are required to maintain the information confidential. All credit card information is transmitted via HTTPS technology. It is also your responsibility to prevent unauthorized access to your account and personal information by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.

    How We Use Personal Information
    We use your personal data we collect from your use of our Website to reply to inquiries, process online, phone, and store transactions, provide status updates on orders, clarify order details, administer surveys or other Website features, such as chat messages, recommend products, services or special offers and promotions and ensure customer satisfaction following a purchase.
    If you have opted-in to receive promotional emails, we may use your email address to send you periodic emails about new products, company news, events and promotions.
    Stealth Sonics retains the information provided by you for as long as your account with Stealth Sonics is in existence or as needed to service you in connection with the products and services offered on our Website.
    We use your personal information to identify theft or fraudulent transactions.
    If you enter into a giveaway, promotional campaign or contest, Stealth Sonics may use your personal information that you provide to administer and publish the results of the giveaway, promotional campaign or contest. We will never give or sell your personal information to 3rd parties for marketing purposes.

    Use of Third Parties to Process Personal Information
    We may contract with third-party service providers to assist us in conducting business with you. These service providers are not permitted to use information about you collected on our behalf except to help us conduct and improve our business and strictly pursuant to instructions by Stealth Sonics.

    Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties
    Stealth Sonics will not disclose your personal data to outside parties unless (a) we provide you with advance notice; (b) we are required to do so pursuant to a judicial or government subpoena, warrant, order or other request or when we are required by law; or (c) we believe that such action is necessary to (i) conform to the law, comply with the legal process served on us or our affiliates, or investigate, prevent or take action regarding suspected or actual illegal activity; (ii) to take precaution against liability, to investigate and defend against third party claims or allegation, to assist government enforcement agencies, or to protect the security or integrity of our Website or our products and promotions, or (iii) to exercise or protect the rights, property , or personal safety of the Company, its employees, agents, or assigns or others. We may disclose your personal information to our subsidiaries and affiliates, contractors, service providers, and other third parties we may use in operation of our Website, conducting Stealth Sonics business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep your personal information confidential.
    Stealth Sonics may also share personal data it collects to a relevant third party in the event of a reorganization, corporate divestiture, merger or sale.

    How to Opt Out of Communications
    At any time, you can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link located at the bottom of each of our promotional emails. Please note that due to email production schedules, you may receive emails already in production.

    How You Can Access, Correct, Update or Request a Copy of Your Personal Data
    We provide you with options related to the collection, use and retention of your personal data. You may access and correct your personal data through your account. If you are unable to correct your personal data through your account, you may email us at marketing@stealthsonics.com. We will take reasonable steps to ensure that inaccurate personal data is deleted or corrected. You may also request a copy of your personal data in machine readable format.

    How Can You Object to the Collection, Retention, Use and Disclosure of Your Personal Data
    You may request that we stop collecting, using and retaining all or some of your personal data if we no longer have a legal right to use your personal data. You may request that we limit the use of your personal data if your personal information is inaccurate, or the processing is unlawful.

    How You Can Request to Have Your Personal Data Deleted
    To delete all of your personal data from our database, sign into your account on our Website and remove your personal data, including your shipping address and billing address. Please note that we may retain your personal data (and information about an individual sales transaction) in order to service that transaction. We may retain personal data even after you have closed your account if necessary to comply with legal obligations (including law enforcement requests), meet regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, or as otherwise permitted by applicable law. We may retain depersonalized data after your account has been closed.

    Children Under the Age of 16
    Our Website is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under age 16. If you are under age 16, do not use or provide any information on our Website or make any purchases through our Website. If we learn we have collected or received personal data from a child under age 16 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information.
