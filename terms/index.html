    The custom IEMs by Stealth Sonics are covered by a one-year limited warranty. This warranty guarantees that Stealth Sonics’ custom IEMs are free from defects in materials and workmanship on its internal components and its external shell. Stealth Sonics will replace or repair the IEMs if found to be defective within the warranty period. Cables included within the product package are covered by a six-months limited warranty. Accessories included in the packaging are as sold as is unless otherwise stated.

    In creating a custom pair of Stealth Sonics IEMs, our audiology partners and Stealth Sonics are thorough in moulding an IEM that is designed to fit perfectly into your ear. However due to several reasons, including the constant evolution of the human ear canal, there may be occurrences where a custom IEM will need to be re-shelled after production. In such instances Stealth Sonics offers a thirty-day refit policy to ensure your custom IEM fits perfectly in your ear. The customer must contact Stealth Sonics, or its service partners, and send the IEM to the service center address within thirty-days post receipt of your order to be covered by the thirty-day refit policy. The customer is responsible for the cost of shipping the IEM to the service centre.
